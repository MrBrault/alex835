Our Story

Sam is something of a neat-freak. He has always wanted things nice and neat and is pretty particular about things being in order. His parents always teased that maybe he should be a housekeeper when he grows up. He took them serious and started working on cleaning houses as a side job. It was only enough to be part-time work until last year when he finally hired a staff and started cleaning houses full time.
